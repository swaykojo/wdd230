Target Audience

Who: Our target is any CEO, business owner, shakeholder and any person that is interested in been part of the group to promote business in the city.

Age: Anyone from 22 to 80.

Education: Anyone who is graduated from high school and has taken the road of entrepreneurship

Income and Motivation: Income above $50,000 annually and be part of a very selected group and stay Updated about activities and event that will held.

Personas

Osei Kwame Despite

Occupation: Businessman

Demographics and Education: Born 2 February 1963 is a Ghanaian entrepreneur and a philanthropist with a foundation in music creation and knowledge of the broadcasting sector. Osei-Kwame is the CEO of the Despite Firm Ltd, a trade company with subsidiaries in the media industry.

Goals and motivation for using the site: wants to promote his business in the city.

Sangu Delle

Demographics and Education: Sangu is CEO of CarePoint (formerly Africa Health Holdings), a tech forward healthcare system operating across the continent, focused on "building Africa's healthcare future." He is also the Founder and Chairman of Golden Palm Investments Corporation ("GPI"); an investment holding company focused on building world class technology companies in Africa. GPI has backed startups such as Andela, mPharma and Flutterwave and GPI portfolio companies have raised over $1.2 billion in venture financing.

Goals and motivation for using the site: wants to identify new business in the city to offer investment and advice.
